Covid Lockdown Comparison of 10 Hardest Hit Countries

A brief look at the Covid Cases in the Top 10 Countries in the world and how the US fared with respect to the rest of the world.

As of July 28th, The US accounted for almost a quarter of the confirmed cases of the entire world. The chart shows when the lockdowns started for most of these countries, including India, the second largest population in the world. Lockdowns were lifted in most of the US right before Memorial Day, even before the number of cases were coming down in the US and other parts of the world. Thus the number of cases increased dramatically.

*Moving the mouse across the chart will show the detailed number of cases and the list will change according to their rank on that particular day.
Data from Jons Hopkins Covid Data Reporsitory (https://github.com/CSSEGISandData/COVID-19)
